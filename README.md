**Your email. Your server. Your rules.**

A self-hosted web email client with AI superpowers — like Gmail, but running on your own hardware.  
Connect any IMAP/SMTP account and get a fast, private, AI-enhanced inbox.  
No cloud. No tracking. No ads. Just your email.
